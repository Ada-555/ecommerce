Orderimo Ltd ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience on our website orderimo.com. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall experience. Cookies are widely used across the internet and are a standard feature of most websites.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable core features such as:

  • Shopping cart functionality (adding items, calculating totals)
  • Secure checkout and payment processing via Stripe
  • Session management (keeping you logged in as you browse)
  • Remembering your preferences (language, currency, store selection)
  • Cookie consent preferences

These cookies cannot be disabled without impairing the functionality of our website. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or filling in forms.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect anonymous information about:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you found our website (search engine, referral link, direct)
  • Which products you viewed or added to your bag
  • Device and browser information (to optimise for your setup)

We use Google Analytics for this purpose. Google Analytics collects data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, but does not identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Payment Cookies

When you make a payment, Stripe (our payment processor) may set cookies to:

  • Process your transaction securely
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Remember your payment preferences for future purchases

These cookies are governed by Stripe's Cookie Policy. We do not have access to or control over these cookies.

Marketing & Advertising Cookies

We may use marketing cookies to:

  • Deliver personalised advertisements based on your browsing behaviour
  • Limit the number of times you see a particular advertisement
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns

These cookies may be set by third-party advertising partners, including Google Ads and social media platforms. We currently do not use marketing cookies on our website, but this may change in the future.

Functional / Preference Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, such as:

  • Your preferred store (Orderimo, PetShop Ireland, DigitalHub)
  • Currency and language preferences
  • Recently viewed products

How Long Do Cookies Last?

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser. Used for temporary storage during a single browsing session.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (days, months, or years) or until manually deleted. Used to remember your preferences across visits.

How to Manage or Disable Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from all or certain websites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close the browser

Instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Digital Advertising Alliance — Ad Choices
Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you see a cookie consent banner. You can click "Accept" to consent to all cookies, or continue browsing with only essential cookies. Your preference is stored in a session cookie and will be remembered for future visits.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please note that:

  • Some features of our website may not work correctly (e.g., shopping cart, checkout)
  • You may need to re-enter your preferences each time you visit
  • Some pages may appear broken or render incorrectly

Updates to This Policy
